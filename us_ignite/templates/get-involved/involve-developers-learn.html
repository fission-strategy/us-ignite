GET INVOLVED - DEVELOPERS - LEARN

The next-generation of Internet is not just about going faster, it is also about getting smarter. By embracing technologies like local-cloud, SDN and gigabit to end-users, you have the potential to build an app that transforms how we receive everything from education to healthcare and more.

Standard web development requires optimizing for speed and minimizing the data that is delivered. Caching and compression introduce buffers and delays. Instead of operating under these constraints - stop compressing, stop buffering, stream data, video, voice - all at once.

1. What can you do with advanced networks?

  • Real-time (apparently instantaneous)
  • Very low latency
  • Reliable (no hiccups)
  • Internet of Things/Cyberphysical interactions
  • Big data to the end user / anchor institution
  • Visual data exploration (“fly-through”)
  • Collaborative (in the moment)
  • Distributed
